Tic Tac Toe

About

This game revolves around putting each others' mark in a 3x3 grid and whoever gets three-in-a-row first wins.

In this version, you get to choose who you want to play with: someone who are willing to play with you if they are next to you (a second player) or a CPU.

The first turn will be determined randomly. However, any subsequent rounds after the first one will have the loser have their turn first.

If you're ready, you may press the button!
